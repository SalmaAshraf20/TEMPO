Calculate your food points

this is a small application that you can calculate your Available food, please first enter your Weight to Calculate your Points, then you can choose from food table and see how many food that you will consume.

Tips and Advice

  1. This diet (Food Point System) depend on the calories, amount of fat and fiber in the food
    • Points = (Calories / 50) + (Fat / 12) - (Fiber / 5)
    • Minimum 20 points per day
    • All you have to do after get your counted points is to divide them during the day into meals
  2. Drink water at least 6-8 glasses a day
  3. Make walking and exercise are an essential part of the day
  4. Eat vegetables and fruits and make them an essential part of your day
  5. Eat foods that contain a lot of fiber
  6. Reducing fat to a maximum of 2 tablespoons per day and reducing white toxins (sugar, salt and white flour)
  7. Staying away from sugars, fats, soft drinks, and starches helps regulate the rate of sugar in the body and lose weight
  8. We start our day by drinking a cup of warm water in the morning with lemon
  9. Unlimited amounts of broccoli, carrots, cucumber, zucchini, lettuce, spinach and vegetable soup are allowed.
  10. Have cheat day per week
